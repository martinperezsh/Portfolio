About Me

Hello my name is Martin Perez, I am a 21 year old from West Valley City, Utah. I graduated from the University of Utah Coding Boot Camp to become a Full Stack Developer in December 2017 and I graduated high school from the Utah Virtual Academy. I chose to become a developer because I believe that computer programming is a great field to work in, now a days everyone uses some sort of computer program in their daily life so programmers are gonna be needed for a long time, and I’ve always loved technology and seeing how it works. I first fell in love with web development after taking a class in high school which ended up being my favorite class I ever ended up taking. In my time programming i’ve learned skills such as HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Materialize, Media Queries, GIT, GitHub, JavaScript, JQuery, PHP, React, JSON, API’s, AJAX, Firebase, Node, MySQL, MongoDB, Express, Sequelize, HandleBars JS, Mongoose, and with more to come!
